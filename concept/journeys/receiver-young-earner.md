# Journey — Young Receiver (global pay-ins)

> Persona: young person in Nigeria who needs to get paid from people/platforms all over the world. Primary wedge for OneDosh growth and post-IDV activation.

---

## Persona: **Tobi**, 19, Lagos

- Edits videos for US YouTubers (DM deals, not agency)
- Small YouTube channel (~8k subs) — AdSense when it clears
- Does freelance graphic design on Twitter/X for international clients
- Plays games, watches PlaywithTomide — heard about OneDosh from a campus friend or stream
- Has never had a "real" dollar account; tried PayPal once, got confused, gave up
- Sends ₦ to his mum sometimes when he has enough
- **Not crypto-native.** Doesn't know what USDC or "Base network" means.

---

## What Tobi is hiring OneDosh for

**Surface:** Get paid from random people/platforms worldwide into one place I control.

**Deeper:** Don't lose the money to freezes, scams, fees, or naira eating it before I'm ready to use it.

**Emotional:** Feel legit — like my work is real money, not a workaround.

---

## Journey map (stages)

| Stage | Tobi's mindset | Job to be done | Failure mode today |
|---|---|---|---|
| **0. Hear about it** | "Another fintech app?" | Trust enough to download | Crowded market, scam fatigue |
| **1. Sign up + IDV** | "Hope this isn't long" | Get verified without rage-quit | Sumsub steps, SSN if US-facing, phone verify |
| **2. Post-IDV moment** | "Okay… now what?" | **Know the one thing to do first** | Empty ₦0 home, Send highlighted, 16 options |
| **3. First receive setup** | "How do I actually get paid?" | Get pay-in details for *my* situation | US account buried under Add → Bank |
| **4. Waiting** | "Did it work?" | Confidence money is coming | No status, anxiety, checks app repeatedly |
| **5. First dollar lands** | "It actually worked" | Feel the win; not get frozen | Freeze on first large credit |
| **6. What now?** | "Do I convert? Spend? Send home?" | Simple next step without jargon | Convert/network/card menu overload |
| **7. Repeat receive** | "Same people pay me again" | Faster path; remembered preferences | Re-enter details every time |
| **8. Protection** | "Someone asked me to refund…" | Not get scammed | No guardrails at send confirm |

## Stage 2 — The critical moment (post-IDV)

**Today:** Wallet ₦0.00 · Send (primary) · Add (secondary) · Do more cards · empty transactions · 5-tab nav.

**Designed first-run:**

> You're verified. Your dollar and naira wallets are open.
> **Who pays you?**
> - Someone abroad (client, brand, fan)
> - A platform (YouTube, TikTok, etc.)
> - I just want to look around

One tap → right receive path. Everything else hidden until relevant.

---

## Stage 3 — Receive paths (by payer type)

### A. Someone abroad (client, brand, fan)

**Tobi needs:** US bank details (ACH) or stablecoin address — and a message to copy-paste.

**System does:**
1. Provision US virtual account (or show stablecoin deposit if payer prefers crypto)
2. Generate "Pay Tobi" block: account, routing, name, amount optional
3. Optional: share link / PDF (already in app for Naira account)

**Tobi sends:** WhatsApp or email to client. Done.

### B. Platform (YouTube AdSense, TikTok, etc.)

**Tobi needs:** Know *which* details to paste where in AdSense/payout settings.

**System does:**
1. "Setting up YouTube payouts?" → step-by-step for OneDosh US account
2. Set watch on account for AdSense-style deposits
3. Explain timing (platform payout cycles)

### C. Multiple payers

Graph learns: Client A (monthly ~$400), Client B (lumpy), YouTube (quarterly). Feed shows expected windows.

---

## Stage 4–5 — Waiting & landing

**Push:** "Payment from [name] is on the way — $X. I'll tell you when it lands."

**On land:** Celebration + clear balance in USD/USDC. Not instant convert unless Tobi chose that.

**Freeze shield (background):** Mark expected income; reduce false-positive holds on first big credit.

---

## Stage 6 — After first receive (graduated home)

Feed cards (not 16 buttons):

1. **Hold** — "You have $X in dollars. Naira dropped 2% this week — keeping it here saved you ~₦Y vs converting on day one." (if applicable)
2. **Spend** — "Pay for Netflix / game pass with your card" (small, concrete)
3. **Convert** — "Need naira for something local? Convert ₦X" (with rate shown)
4. **Send home** — "Send ₦ to Mum" (when ready)

Tobi confirms each action. No silent moves.

---

## Stage 7 — Repeat (compounding)

- Saved pay-in details per payer type
- "Your usual client usually pays around now — resend payment details?"
- Platform payout predicted from history
- Less IDV-like friction every time

---

## Stage 8 — Protection (earn trust forever)

At send/convert/refund request:

- Scam pattern interrupt (overpayment refund, urgency, new account)
- Explain in plain language, not compliance speak
- Pause + verify options

---

## Success metrics (this journey)

| Metric | Definition |
|---|---|
| **TTFR** | Time from IDV complete → first successful receive |
| **Setup completion** | % who get pay-in details and share/send them within 24h |
| **D7 after first receive** | Second session, second action (convert/spend/send) |
| **Freeze rate** | First-receive holds / support tickets |
| **Scam saves** | Interrupted suspicious sends (later) |
