- Geographic expansion: US, Nigeria, Canada, 29 European countries

## Who they're acquiring

**Skew:** Young, mobile-native, **Nigeria-based**, receive-oriented:
- Creators, gamers, students, freelancers
- Less (so far) classic diaspora "send money home" family providers — though Cash App targets that

**Implication:** Growth machine optimizes **signups**, not **first receive**. Post-IDV activation is the leaky bucket.

---

## Infra & trust signals

- Partners: Visa, Plaid, Fireblocks
- Team pedigree: ZeroHash, Plaid, Amazon
- CBN **Mobile Money Operator (MMO) license**
- ~100K+ Play installs; 4.0 App Store / 3.8 Play (early reviews)

---

## Marketing promise vs product gap

Website promises: no hidden fees, freeze/unfreeze, real fraud prevention, funds stay protected.

Product (screenshots): passive dashboard, ₦0 empty state, 16+ choices post-IDV, Send emphasized over Add.

**The gap is the opportunity** — deliver the protection story through agentic/first-run experience.
